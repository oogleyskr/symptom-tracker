# Privacy Policy — Symptom Tracker AI

**Last updated: March 27, 2026**

## Overview

Symptom Tracker AI ("the App") is designed to help you track symptoms, medications, and health patterns. Your privacy matters — here's how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Health logs**: Symptoms, medications, side effects, and notes you enter
- **App preferences**: Theme settings, onboarding status, notification preferences

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identity information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or crash reports (unless you opt in)

## Data Storage

- **All health data is stored locally on your device** using an encrypted SQLite database
- Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export a report
- No cloud sync, no server uploads, no third-party data sharing

## AI Processing

- Symptom pattern analysis runs **entirely on-device** using local heuristics
- No health data is sent to external AI services
- Future Gemini Nano integration will also process on-device

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Play Billing**: Used for Pro subscription processing. Google handles payment data per their privacy policy. We do not store payment information.
- No other third-party SDKs or analytics services are used.

## Data Export

- You may export health reports as PDF files via the share function
- Exported data is shared only with apps/contacts you explicitly choose
- We have no access to exported reports

## Data Deletion

- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data
- There is no cloud backup to clean up

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be noted in the app's update changelog on the Play Store.
