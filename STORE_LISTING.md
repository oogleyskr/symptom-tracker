## Full Description

**Take control of your health — finally.**

SymptomTracker AI is the simplest way to log your symptoms, track your medications, and understand what's actually happening with your health. No complex medical jargon. No bloated features. Just fast logging and smart AI analysis.

**🩺 Log in seconds**
Tap a symptom, drag a severity slider, done. Rate how you're feeling from 1-10 and add notes if you want. Medication taken? One tap. No friction, no forms.

**🧠 AI that actually helps**
Gemini AI analyzes your logs and surfaces real insights:
- Are your symptoms trending better or worse?
- What's your most frequent symptom?
- Do your symptoms spike after certain medications?
- What's your medication adherence looking like?

**📊 See your health visually**
Clean charts show symptom severity trends and medication adherence over the past 7 days. Spot patterns your doctor might miss.

**📋 Doctor-ready reports**
Heading to an appointment? Generate a clean health summary and share it in one tap. No more trying to remember what happened three weeks ago.

**🔒 Your data, your device**
All data is stored locally on your device by default. No cloud, no servers, no sharing without your permission.
